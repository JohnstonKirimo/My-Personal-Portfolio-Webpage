My Story

Born in the coastal region of Kilifi, Kenya. Went to Bogamachuko Primary School where I completed my KCPE. Pursued my highschool education in Nairobi at Lenana School before heading back to Mombasa where I completed the International Baccalaurette (IB) Diploma at the AgaKhan Academy Mombasa. Afterwards, I travelled to Vancouver, Canada, where I did my undergraduate degree in the faculty of Commerce at the Sauder School of Business.

Currently, my career interests are in Business and Software Development. My goal is to eventualy go back to Kilifi and help fellow young people to start their own businesses so as to attain financial freedom and contribute towards economic development of the country.
